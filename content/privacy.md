# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 30, 2026*

## Who We Are

Self-e-Tape is operated by Philip Riccio ("we," "us," "our"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Self-e-Tape mobile application and website (selfetape.com).

## What We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account (via Apple Sign In), we collect:
- Your name (as provided by Apple)
- Your email address (or Apple's relay email if you choose to hide your email)

### Scripts and Recordings
- Scripts you import are processed **on your device only**. We do not upload, store, or access your scripts on our servers.
- Video and audio recordings are stored **on your device only**. We do not upload, store, or access your recordings unless you explicitly choose to share or export them.

### Usage Data
We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app:
- Feature usage frequency
- App crashes and error reports
- Device type and iOS version

We do **not** collect:
- The content of your scripts
- Your audio or video recordings
- Your performance data

### Residuals Program
If you participate in the Residuals Program, we collect:
- Your referral code usage
- Earnings and payout information
- Payment details (processed securely through Stripe)

### Email Signup
If you join our mailing list, we collect your email address and optionally your name.

## How We Use Your Information

- **Account management:** To manage your subscription and app access
- **App improvement:** Anonymous analytics help us fix bugs and improve features
- **Communications:** To send you updates about Self-e-Tape (only if you opt in)
- **Residuals Program:** To track referrals and process payments

## What We Don't Do

- We do **not** sell your personal information
- We do **not** use your data for AI training
- We do **not** share your information with advertisers
- We do **not** access your scripts, recordings, or creative work

## Data Storage and Security

- All script and recording data stays on your device
- Account data is stored securely on Supabase (US East region)
- Payment processing is handled by Stripe and Apple (we never see your full payment details)
- We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Apple Sign In:** Authentication
- **Supabase:** Account data storage
- **RevenueCat:** Subscription management
- **Stripe:** Residuals Program payments
- **ElevenLabs:** Text-to-speech for AI reader voices
- **SendGrid:** Email communications

Each service has its own privacy policy and data handling practices.

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Access** your data by contacting us
- **Delete** your account and all associated data by contacting us
- **Opt out** of marketing emails at any time
- **Request** a copy of your data

## Children

Self-e-Tape is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
